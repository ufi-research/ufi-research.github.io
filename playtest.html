Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to test?
Depending on the requirements of the company, the study may be a video game, a website, an application, or another product
How do I qualify for a test?
You must meet the requirements that are set forth by the supplier of the initial study such as the company that hired us or the owner of the game you'll be playing
How do I become a tester?
Click on the button labled "Join Our Playtest Server" above, accept the invitation to join the server, and follow the instructions in the "#Welcome" channel
Can I tell my friends about the product I tested?
It will depend on each study, but generally you cannot share your involvement during the study with anyone (as clarified in the Non-Disclosure Agreement you sign before participating) - but oftentimes, the NDA becomes null after the release of the product
In-person or remote?
It will depend on the study; some products will require an in-person test, but most will be conducted remotely. Availability for in-person testing will be confirmed before participant selection. All in-person testing will take place in Orlando, Florida
How old do I need to be to test?
Anyone at least 18 years of age is eligible to join the playtest pool!
